BARANGAY
    SAN VICENTE
Barangay San Vicente is a vibrant community known for its friendly atmosphere and close-knit residents. Nestled in a picturesque location, it boasts a blend of traditional and modern amenities. The area features well-maintained parks, bustling markets, and a variety of local businesses that cater to the daily needs of its inhabitants. Educational institutions and healthcare facilities are also conveniently accessible, contributing to the overall quality of life. With its welcoming environment and community spirit, Barangay San Vicente is a charming place to live and visit.The community actively participates in various cultural and social activities, fostering a strong sense of unity and belonging among its members. Festivals and local events are regularly celebrated, reflecting the rich heritage and traditions of Barangay San Vicente. The barangay hall serves as a hub for civic engagement, where residents come together to discuss and address communal concerns, ensuring the continuous development and improvement of the area.
MISSION

BARANGAY SAN VICENTE shall spearhead the massive implementation of various community program, projects and activities to its constituents, enhancing the active participation of all non-government organizations community-building, mobilizing its resources with in promoting the general well-being of its people for a progressive and peaceful community to live in.
VISION

BARANGAY SAN VICENTE as the economic and commercial seat of the City of Ilagan home for morally upright and healthy citizenry living in an ecologically balanced environment with a progressive and stable economy lead by righteous leadership.

Demographics

Households
The household population of San Vicente in the 2015 Census was 2,439 broken down into 545 households or an average of 4.48 members per household.

Population by age group

According to the 2015 Census, the age group with the highest population in San Vicente is 20 to 24, with 248 individuals. Conversely, the age group with the lowest population is 70 to 74, with 20 individuals.

Combining age groups together, those aged 14 and below, consisting of the young dependent population which include infants/babies, children and young adolescents/teenagers, make up an aggregate of 28.37% (692). Those aged 15 up to 64, roughly, the economically active population and actual or potential members of the work force, constitute a total of 66.95% (1,633). Finally, old dependent population consisting of the senior citizens, those aged 65 and over, total 4.67% (114) in all.

The computed Age Dependency Ratios mean that among the population of San Vicente, there are 42 youth dependents to every 100 of the working age population; there are 7 aged/senior citizens to every 100 of the working population; and overall, there are 49 dependents (young and old-age) to every 100 of the working population.

The median age of 26 indicates that half of the entire population of San Vicente are aged less than 26 and the other half are over the age of 26.

Historical population

The population of San Vicente fell from 3,028 in 1990 to 2,214 in 2020, a decrease of 814 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a negative growth rate of 2.02%, or a decrease of 225 people, from the previous population of 2,439 in 2015.

Location

San Vicente is situated at approximately 17.1433, 121.8891, in the island of Luzon. Elevation at these coordinates is estimated at 50.0 meters or 164.0 feet above mean sea level.
